Work for us

We are a great bunch...promise

Our philosophy is to enrich the members lives, through training, socials and adventure, the same applies for our team. We are always looking for great people from trainers, to marketing and recruitment.

If you are interested in any positions we would love to hear from you, just email jobs@one-element.co.uk and we will get you down to the Crewroom for a coffee.

Why work for One Element?

Our trainers come from all walks of life. We have professional sportsmen/women, personal trainers, sports coaches, teachers, surveyors, graduates and even solicitors as our trainers. The success of your location comes from your ability to engage, be fun and play with the group.

Over the past 6 years we have developed a unique training programme built by professional sportsmen/women as well as a unique management system that allows you a profit share in your location(s).

One Element trainer/ staff benefits

Structured sessions, developed alongside the UK’s leading sports and fitness coaches, are provided each week. Quarterly developmental meetings to work on the psychology, coaching and fitness skills that we pride ourselves on Opportunities to organise, attend, support or lead expeditions and trips to all corners of the globe.

What does being a One-Element trainer involve?

Trainers will be working with groups of up to 25 members, delivering high intensity sessions developed by head trainers at OE. You will be working with new members developing goals and involving them with the group and socials. Trainers need to be able to think on their feet with games and programmes for less confident members and must continually engage the group’s members to enter all the extra OE activities.

Finally, to ensure we continue to improve, we ask and take feedback from members after all sessions and review with trainers quarterly.
