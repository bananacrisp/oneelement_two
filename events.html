Our Events

join us on our many adventures

Wild Swim

We do wild river swims at Henley twice a year, there is an element of teaching and an opportunity to become comfortable with open water in a safe environment.

Skiing

Every year since 2008 we have taken groups of 8 to 32 out to the French town of Morzine to ski the Portes de soliel. We even throw heli-skiing into the mix too.

Stand Up Paddlboarding

We run monthly SUP sessions from our HQ on the thames near Putney Bridge. This is a easy way to get on the water and enjoy the serene view from the river

Summer Sports

We do a summer holiday every year to surf, windsurf, party. Every night we play games and party, every day we play on the water and enjoy the sun.

Touch rugby

Saturday 9:30am beginners touch, were we teach the basic & fun aspects of touch. Sunday 11am this is a drop in game for anyone who is a member of One Element, up to two hours long.

Party Time

We are famed for our Huge Halloween party's | Christmas party's | spring party's. Some times with fancy dress the highlight of the nights events.
