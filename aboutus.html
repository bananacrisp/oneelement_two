About Us

Our approach

Based on bringing the social and fitness training from sports clubs to the public, One Element’s training programme is very unique.

A number of top sports players from England Women’s Rugby and top sports coaches helped to build a bespoke training program, where every week is different but run consistently through every location by all the trainer.

The programme is made up of 26 training sessions that work through a cycle of speed, strength and endurance to deliver progressive gains.

Designed for all

Men and women of all fitness levels and ages are welcome at the sessions and qualified instructors tailor exercises to different members to ensure individuals are working to their own limits.

Classes are designed to get the heart pumping and no two classes are the same, ensuring regular participants don’t know what to expect next and constantly receive varied sessions.

The classes

Classes last for one hour and consist of a small group of like-minded people all working together, both in competition and as teams with a real sense of fun.

Each class is taken by fully qualified, knowledgeable sports coach or athletes who constantly motivate and encourage participants while ensuring exercises are performed properly.

Exercises include sprinting, paired-exercises and a few energetic team games.

Origins

Set up in 2006, and now in its 8th year, One Element has 11 outdoor fitness venues across London. Tom Marien launched One Element, after he realised that the positive values from a sports club should be accessible to the public and not just people who play sport at the weekend.
